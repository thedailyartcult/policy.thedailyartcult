This Refund Policy was last updated on June 21, 2026.

This policy explains how The Daily Art Cult handles cancellations and refund requests for subscriptions processed through Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Please read it carefully before subscribing.

Because our Services deliver immediate access to personalized digital content — including bespoke audiobooks generated specifically for you — our refund policy is intentionally conservative. We offer a free trial precisely so you can experience the platform before committing.

Section 01

Our Position on Refunds

The Daily Art Cult operates on an annual subscription model at USD $49.00 per year. Upon subscribing, you receive immediate and full access to all platform features for the duration of your subscription year.

General rule: We do not offer refunds for annual subscription payments once the free trial period has ended and the subscription charge has been processed. This applies regardless of how much of the subscription period has been used.

This position reflects the nature of our product: bespoke audio content is generated and delivered personally to you. Once created, it cannot be returned. The free trial exists to give you every opportunity to evaluate the platform before payment is required.

Section 02

Free Trial

All new subscribers receive a free trial period before their subscription begins. During this period:

  • No charge applies. Your payment method is on file but will not be charged until the trial period ends.
  • Full access is granted. You have complete access to the Services during the trial, exactly as you would as a paying subscriber.
  • Cancel anytime, no cost. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged. Your access will continue until the trial period expires.
  • Automatic conversion. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert and your payment method will be charged USD $49.00 for the annual period.

You will receive a reminder notification before your trial converts to a paid subscription. It is your responsibility to cancel before conversion if you do not wish to be charged.

Section 03

After the Trial Ends

Once the free trial has ended and the annual subscription charge has been successfully processed, the payment is final and non-refundable under our standard policy.

  • Partial refunds for unused months are not offered. The annual subscription is priced as a single commitment, not a monthly rate.
  • Cancellation after payment does not entitle you to a refund. It stops your subscription from renewing at the next annual cycle. You retain full access for the remainder of the paid period.
  • Non-use of the platform after subscribing does not constitute grounds for a refund.
Section 04

Exceptions

We review refund requests on a case-by-case basis in exceptional circumstances. We may, at our sole discretion, issue a full or partial refund in the following situations:

  • Duplicate charge: If you were charged more than once for the same subscription period due to a technical error.
  • Service failure: If a documented technical failure on our part prevented you from accessing the Services for a material portion of your subscription period and the issue was not resolved within a reasonable time after being reported to us.
  • Unauthorized transaction: If you did not authorize the subscription charge and reported it to us within 7 days of the charge appearing.
Dissatisfaction with content — including a dislike of the philosophical approach, audio style, or narration voice — is not grounds for a refund. This is precisely what the free trial is designed to address before commitment.

All exception requests must be submitted in writing to support@thedailyartcult.lol within 14 days of the charge in question.

Section 05

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewal charges but does not trigger a refund for the current period.

  • How to cancel: Through your account settings, or by emailing support@thedailyartcult.lol.
  • When it takes effect: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current annual billing period. You retain full access until that date.
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email when your cancellation is processed. If you do not receive one within 48 hours, contact us.
Section 06

Chargebacks

All payment disputes are handled by Paddle as our Merchant of Record. If you believe a charge was unauthorized or erroneous, we ask that you contact us directly at support@thedailyartcult.lol before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer.

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in the immediate suspension of your account. We will provide full cooperation and documentation to your payment provider in the event of a dispute investigation.

Section 07

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund under the exceptions outlined in Section 4, submit your request as follows:

  • Email support@thedailyartcult.lol with the subject line: Refund Request — [your account email]
  • Include your account email address, the date of the charge, and a brief description of the reason for your request.
  • Requests must be submitted within 14 days of the charge in question.

We will respond within 5 business days. If a refund is approved, it will be processed through Paddle and may take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement depending on your payment method and bank.
